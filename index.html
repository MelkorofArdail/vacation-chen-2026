BASE 1 · AUG 13–15 · 3 NIGHTS

🌊 Hévíz, Hungary

Europe's largest thermal lake, Lake Balaton day trip, and the Festetics Palace — a gentle warm start after landing.

💡
Getting here: Land Budapest (BUD), pick up rental car (5-seat wagon/SUV), drive ~2h to Hévíz. Buy the Hungarian e-matrica motorway vignette online before the trip. Grab groceries at a Spar on the way in.
Thu · Aug 13

Land Budapest → Drive to Hévíz

DRIVE2:00to apartment

Land at BUD, collect car, stop at a Tesco/Spar for supplies, arrive Hévíz early evening. Easy first night — dinner from the apartment, early bed after the flight.

  • Car rental pickup: Budapest Airport (BUD) Arrivals
  • Buy motorway vignette at the fuel station or online
  • Grocery stop: Aldi/Spar en route on the M7
Navigate to Hévíz
Arrival day🕘 Check-in from 15:00
Fri · Aug 14

Lake Hévíz + Festetics Palace

WALK5min to lake

Morning float in the famous warm thermal lake — the water is 22–38°C, black mineral-rich, and unique in Europe. Shallow roped zone great for Aviv. Afternoon: drive 10 min to Keszthely for the baroque Festetics Palace and its stunning Helikon Library.

  • Lake Hévíz: adult ~€19.50 / child ~€10 / Aviv free — family ~€59
  • Festetics Palace: adult ~€12 / child ~€6 — family ~€42
  • Bring water shoes for the lake (muddy bottom at edges)
  • Ave time: 2–3h lake + 1–1.5h palace
If it rains: The indoor thermal pool section of Hévíz is fully covered. Lake Hévíz
Paid💧 Thermal🕘 Lake: 09:00–17:30
Sat · Aug 15

Balaton Beach + Tihany Peninsula

DRIVE50min (Siófok)

South shore of Lake Balaton — shallow, warm, and extends far out, ideal for a 5-year-old. Pedal boats, beach bars, playground. On the way back stop at the dramatic Tihany Peninsula — abbey views, lavender fields, and the famous echo hill.

  • South shore beach: free access, lifeguards in July–Aug
  • Tihany Abbey views: free / abbey entry ~€4 adult
  • Pack lunch from apartment — beach food is pricey
  • Kids' favourite: pedal boat rental ~€10/30min
If it rains: Festetics Palace (if missed yesterday) or chill morning in the apartment + Hévíz town walk.
Free beach💧 Lake swim

BASE 2 · AUG 16–19 · 4 NIGHTS

♨️ Moravske Toplice, Slovenia

Terme 3000 water park on the doorstep, a day trip into Austria for the Graz mountain slide, and medieval Ptuj.

💡
Drive from Hévíz: ~1h 45min west. Buy the Slovenian e-vignette (eVinjeta.si) before entering Slovenia — mandatory on all motorways. The Terme 3000 area is in Prekmurje, Slovenia's northeast corner.
Sun · Aug 16

Drive to Moravske Toplice + Terme 3000 first splash

DRIVE1:45from Hévíz

Smooth morning drive into Slovenia. Check in early afternoon. Spend the rest of the day getting acquainted with Terme 3000 — pick your favourite pools and slides before the big day on Aug 19.

  • Terme 3000: 22 indoor/outdoor pools, black thermal water, slide complex
  • Buy Slovenian e-vignette (eVinjeta.si) before the border
  • Mercator supermarket 2 min from the resort for groceries
Navigate to Moravske Toplice
💧 Thermal🕘 Terme: 09:00–21:00
Mon · Aug 17

Graz Day Trip — Schlossberg Slide!

DRIVE1:28each way

Austria day! Drive to Graz for the Schlossbergrutsche — a 64m slide built inside a mountain, the world's tallest indoor slide. Noga and Inbar will go again and again. Then the Schlossberg clock tower funicular for panoramic rooftop views over the red-tiled city. Walk the old town for lunch.

  • Schlossbergrutsche slide: ~€6.50 adult / ~€4 child — family ~€25
  • Funicular up: ~€3.50 adult / ~€2 child
  • Lunch: Hauptplatz or Lendplatz area (~€12–15/head)
  • Buy Austrian digital vignette (asfinag.at) before the border
  • ⚠️ Graz is worth 4–5 hours — leave by 08:30, back by 18:00
If it rains: Graz museums are great — Kunsthaus (art museum) or the Naturkundemuseum. The slide is INDOOR anyway!
Schlossbergrutsche, Graz
Paid🇦🇹 Austria day
Tue · Aug 18

Vinarium Tower + Ptuj Old Town

DRIVE28min (Lendava)

Morning: drive 28 min south to the Vinarium Tower in Lendava — a 53m wine-region lookout where you can see 4 countries (Slovenia, Hungary, Croatia, Austria) at once. Kids love the elevator + the "I see 4 countries!" moment. Afternoon: swing through Ptuj, Slovenia's oldest town — a compact, photogenic hilltop castle and lovely pedestrian old town.

  • Vinarium Tower: ~€7 adult / ~€5 child — family ~€24. Mon closed.
  • Ptuj Castle: ~€5 adult / ~€3 child — free to walk the old town
  • Lunch at a gostilna (inn) in Ptuj — try prekmurska gibanica (layered pastry)
Vinarium Tower, Lendava
Paid4 countries view!
Wed · Aug 19

Full Terme 3000 Water Park Day

WALK2min

Full day at Terme 3000 — the whole complex from open to late afternoon. Every slide, every pool. The thermal black-water pools in the evening are magical. Pack sunscreen, a change of clothes, and snacks.

  • Go early to claim sun loungers before it fills up
  • All slides open from 10:00 — lap the big ones with the kids
  • Lunch: on-site restaurant or bring a packed lunch (lockers available)
  • Thermal pools at 6PM when the day-trippers leave — pure bliss
💧 Full water park day🕘 09:00–21:00

BASE 3 · AUG 20–25 · 6 NIGHTS

🏔️ Bled, Slovenia

The scenic crown of the trip — island boat rides, cliff-top castle, Alpine gorges, toboggan run, and Austria just over the hills.

⚠️
Book before you go: Postojna Cave tours sell out — buy online. Vogel Cable Car is weather-dependent. Vintgar Gorge: arrive before 09:30 in August to avoid queues.
Thu · Aug 20

Drive to Bled + Lake Stroll + Kremšnita

DRIVE2:00from Moravske

Arrive Bled by early afternoon. Walk the lake promenade (4.7km loop — easy with Aviv), watch the pletna boats on the water, and have your first kremšnita at the Parkhotel café. That cream cake is non-negotiable.

  • Lake loop: flat, fully paved, stroller-friendly
  • Kremšnita: ~€5 each at Parkhotel Bled café — it's the original
  • Grocery stop: Mercator Bled (3 min drive from most apartments)
Navigate to Bled
Free lake stroll🕘 Check-in from 15:00
Fri · Aug 21

Pletna Boat to Bled Island + Bled Castle

DRIVE5min to dock

Morning: take a traditional pletna flat-boat to Bled Island — the iconic church visit, climb the 99 steps, ring the wishing bell (every family member gets a wish!). Afternoon: hike or drive up to Bled Castle for the best aerial view of the lake + museum visit.

  • Pletna boat: ~€20 adult / €10 child (4–12) / Aviv ~€10 — cash only on boat — family ~€70
  • Bled Castle: ~€15 adult / ~€6 child (6–15) / Aviv free — family ~€42
  • ⚠️ Bring cash for the pletna — the boatmen don't take cards
  • Castle printing press demo: kids love it, included in entry
If it rains: Skip the boat — the castle is covered and the views are dramatic in moody weather.
Bled Island Boat Dock
PaidThe Bled experience
Sat · Aug 22

Lake Bohinj + Vogel Cable Car

DRIVE30min west

Quieter, wilder Lake Bohinj inside Triglav National Park — turquoise glacier water, mountain backdrop, and you can wild-swim right from the shore. Then take the Vogel cable car to 1535m altitude for big, sweeping Julian Alps panoramas — the best mountain views of the whole trip.

  • Vogel cable car: ~€33 adult / €16 child (6–14) / Aviv ~€8 — family ~€90
  • Lake Bohinj swimming: free — beach area near the boat dock
  • Pack warm layer for the cable car (mountain wind at 1535m)
  • Combine with a picnic lunch on the lakeshore
If cable car closed (weather): Walk the Bohinj lakeside trail (3km easy loop) and swim all day instead.
Lake Bohinj / Vogel Cable Car
Cable car paidLake swim free
Sun · Aug 23

Vintgar Gorge (early!) + Straža Toboggan

DRIVE12min (gorge)

Start at Vintgar Gorge before 09:30 — a stunning 1.6km boardwalk over emerald water. Crowds arrive after 10am. Back for lunch, then afternoon at Straža summer toboggan run on the Bled hillside — Aviv rides tandem with you, Noga and Inbar can solo.

  • Vintgar Gorge: ~€10 adult / €5 child (6–15) / Aviv free — family ~€30
  • Straža toboggan: ~€14/ride adult, ~€11 child — 3-ride bundle saves
  • ⚠️ Aviv needs hand-holding on narrow gorge sections
  • Bring water — the gorge walk is warm and humid in August

Mon · Aug 24

Austria Day — Wörthersee + Minimundus

DRIVE1:15each way

Cross into Austria for a double-header at Klagenfurt lake: Wörthersee warm Alpine lake swim (~26°C in August) then Minimundus miniature world — 170 scale models of world monuments, the kids will spot things they know. Both are on the same lakeshore.

  • Minimundus: ~€21 adult / ~€11 child (4–14) / Aviv free — family ~€64
  • Wörthersee swim: free at public beaches (Strandbad ~€5 entry)
  • Lunch at the Wörthersee lakeside — eat early to beat queues
  • Austrian vignette already bought (Graz day) — no extra cost
Wörthersee / Minimundus, Klagenfurt

Flex Day — Postojna Cave OR Ljubljana OR Lake Day

DRIVE55–70min either way

Pick one based on energy and weather:

  • 🦎 Postojna Cave (70 min) — electric train through the cave, 10°C inside, book ahead. Adult ~€28.80 / child ~€19.50 / Aviv €1 — family ~€78

  • 🏊 Lazy Bled lake day — Bled lake swim + kremšnita round 2 + rental SUP boards. Budget day.
If it rains: Postojna Cave is the perfect rainy option — fully underground, 100% weather-proof. Book now at postojnska-jama.eu

  • Stop at Balaton south shore for a stretch break (~1h30 in)

Aquaworld Full Day

DRIVE20min north

Budapest's biggest water park — 11 slides, wave pool, lazy river, indoor and outdoor sections. This is the grand water-park finale of the trip. Book tickets online to guarantee entry in August.

  • Aquaworld: ~€33 adult / ~€20 child (4–14) / Aviv free — family ~€106
  • ⚠️ Book online — August is peak, can sell out at the gate
  • Bring flip-flops and a dry bag for valuables
  • Lazy river + wave pool — all ages covered

Fri · Aug 28

Buda Castle + Fisherman's Bastion + Széchenyi Baths

DRIVE10min to Buda

Morning on the Buda hill: neo-Gothic Fisherman's Bastion (best Danube + Parliament view in the city), Matthias Church, and the Castle District. Afternoon: cross to Pest for the iconic Széchenyi yellow palace baths — outdoor thermal pools, chess players, unforgettable Budapest moment.

  • Fisherman's Bastion terraces: free (tower entry ~€3 adult)
  • Széchenyi Baths: ~€35 adult / ~€20 child — family ~€110
  • Buda funicular (optional): ~€5 up, or walk the free steps
  • Bring swimwear + flip-flops for the baths

Margaret Island + Market Hall + Final Dinner
